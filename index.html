Welcome to my domain

I am a CS PhD Student at University of Virginia
My research focuses on analyzing large scale human mobility data. Currently, I am using this mobility data to explain disease dynamics and movment patterns. My research advisor is Madhav Marathe.

I completed my B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).
