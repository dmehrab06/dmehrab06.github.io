Welcome to my domain

I am a CS PhD Student at University of Virginia
My research focuses on analyzing large scale human mobility data. Currently, I am using this mobility data to explain disease dynamics and movment patterns. My research advisor is Madhav Marathe.

I completed my B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).

My Music

In my free time, I listen to music and try to cover the ones I like in my guitar. In terms of guitar playing skill, I consider myself a little above noob but below amateur. I am recently opting for fingerstyle covers and it is killing me. But, there is also that feeling of elation if I do manage to cover something successfully

My Anime

I have been watching anime since 2009 and have watched quite a handful of them. Some of my favourites are: Death Note, Code Geass, Steins; Gate, Full Metal Alchemist , Tatami Galaxy. I am also a huge fan of One Piece. I love discussing One Piece theories with people. If you are a One Piece fan also and have some theories, I will be happy to hear it anytime. My favourite anime characters are Nico Robin from One Piece and Itachi Uchiha from Naruto. I like characters that are fleshed out, not characters who look cool in appearance. If you just like an anime character because of his/her appearance, I probably will have an argument with you and not respect your opinion.

My Pictures

Another of my pastime involves drawing different characters. I can also draw stencil of people's faces given a reference. However, even if you did receive a sketch of yours, there is no need to think that I am your bestie or something. It's just one of my way of saying thanks.
